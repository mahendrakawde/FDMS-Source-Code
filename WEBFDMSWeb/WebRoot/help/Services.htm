Services

Use this tab to enter all the details about the service including (if applicable) Pall Bearer contact information.

When working with this tab, here are some things to not:

  • When you see the Triangle  icon, click it to view the Speed Data available for this field.

  • If Pall Bearers are used, click the Pall Bearers button to display the Pall Bearers window in which you enter all the contact information.  Click Save when finished.

  • When all selections/entries are made, click Save.
