Vitals

Use this tab to enter all the details required for the Death Certificate.

NOTE:  Certain states have Certificate of Death forms already setup in the system.  If you happen to be in one of those states, you will see the applicable form for your state versus a generic vitals form.  If you happen to see a generic vitals form, have the system administrator check the Location Management function to make sure "Generic Vitals" is not checked.

Most of the fields are self-explanatory.  However, here are some things to note about certain other fields:

  • Relevant Dates & Ages: Under 1 Year/1 Day.  If the Decedent has not attained 1 year or 1 day of age, complete these fields.

  • General History: Social Security Number.  When entering the number, do not type the dashes (-) as they are inserted automatically.

  • Marital Status: Surviving Spouse.  If the spouse is female, give her name prior to being married to the decedent.

When all selections/entries are made, click Save.
