Financials

Use this tab to enter all the financial information related to this case.

Most of the fields are self-explanatory.  However, here are some details for selected fields:

  • Send Charges to Accounting.  Sends it to the system so payment can be processed as well as allowing you to send the entries to your third-party accounting software.

  • Last Stmnt Date.  This date is generated by the system.

In addition, a series of buttons are provided across the top that directly relate to the financial data for this case.

  • Add Services

  • Add Merchandise

  • Insert Package

  • Specify Components

  • Change Price Lists

  • Bill To Parties

  • Inventory.  Click this button to launch the Inventory Manager.

When all selections/entries are made, click Save.

NOTES ABOUT SERVICES. MERCHANDISE & PACKAGES

Once you have added a service, merchandise item or package to your case, you can modify the information (price, tax code, exempted amount, and G/L account) by doing the following:

  1. Locate the service, merchandise, or package item listing on the Financials tab.

  2. Click the hyperlink for the item.  The Change Contract Charge window displays.

  3. Make the necessary changes.

  4. Click the Save button.
